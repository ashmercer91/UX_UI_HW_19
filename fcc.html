FCC Website Redesign

The Problem

The FCC website is challenging to navigate, resulting in users not being able to find and take advantage of the resources they need.

The Goal

Simplify the complexity of the FCC website for better accessibility and to make it easier for users to find what they need.

Our Team

Carolyn Fraiser - UX/UI Designer Ashley Mercer - UX/UI Designer

Tools Used
  • Figma
  • Miro
  • PowerPoint
  • Invision
The Plan

We were given the challenge of making the FCC website more user friendly. We started by testing the current site in order to find pain points and frustrations. Due to the size of the website, we decided to dedicate our focus on the consumer side of the site.





Initial Testing and Feedback

We conducted four intial tests where we asked users to start at the FCC home page and navigate to different areas of the consumer section. We quickly found some real issues with navigation, including:

  • Main sections of the consumer page are hidden and hard to find.
  • Many links are broken and the search bar doesn't always work
  • multiple pages have similar titles with different objectives, making it very confusing.
